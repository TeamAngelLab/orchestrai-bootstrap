## Regulated Industry Value Propositions

OrchestrAI™ is purpose-built for industries where AI mistakes are not recoverable.

### 🏦 Financial Services & Banking
SOX/SEC compliance demands deterministic, auditable AI decisions. OrchestrAI™'s Forensic Ledger provides board-grade replay of every agent action. The S.A.F.E. Gate ensures no AI transaction executes below 90% confidence, eliminating hallucinated trades or advice.
**Compliance:** SOX · SEC · Basel III · FINRA · AML/KYC

### 🏥 Healthcare & Life Sciences
HIPAA and FDA 21 CFR Part 11 require immutable audit trails for AI touching patient data. OrchestrAI™'s PII Airlock Shield (L4) enforces privacy-first behavior at the infrastructure level. Proven via BRAHM: 23 agents, India ONDC, HIPAA-compliant in production.
**Compliance:** HIPAA · FDA 21 CFR · HL7 FHIR · GDPR

### 🛡 Insurance
Insurance underwriting and claims require explainable AI — regulators across 50 US states mandate algorithmic decision transparency. Full decision audit trail, confidence scoring on every recommendation, kill-switch governance layer.
**Compliance:** NAIC · State DOI · IFRS 17 · Solvency II

### 🎓 Education
FERPA and COPPA mandate strict controls over AI systems interacting with students. OrchestrAI™'s PII Airlock Shield vaults sensitive student data before it reaches any LLM — making Claude deployable where raw LLM deployments are blocked by legal teams.
**Compliance:** FERPA · COPPA · WCAG 2.1

### 🔐 Cybersecurity
AI-powered threat detection requires zero tolerance for false positives. EVOLUTION-X (L21) continuously red-teams the stack before reaching production. Simulate-before-act protocol — no action fires without shadow execution.
**Compliance:** NIST CSF · SOC 2 · ISO 27001 · FedRAMP

### ⚖ Legal & Compliance
Legal AI must be explainable to regulators, judges, and bar associations. Full provenance on every AI-generated output. Multi-agent consensus validation reduces hallucination to near-zero for contract review and regulatory filing automation.
**Compliance:** ABA Model Rules · GDPR Art.22 · E-Discovery standards

### 🪖 Defense & Government
Sovereign AI is a national security requirement. OrchestrAI™'s Sovereign Stack runs entirely on-premise or GovCloud with zero data egress by design. Ouroboros Protocol enables air-gapped operation for classified deployments.
**Compliance:** FedRAMP · ITAR · CMMC · DoD IL5

### 🪙 Crypto, Fintech & Digital Assets
Fintech Agent (L22) built for high-frequency autonomous execution with pre-commit simulation. The Forensic Ledger provides the immutable audit log satisfying SEC, FinCEN, and MiCA requirements simultaneously.
**Compliance:** MiCA · FinCEN · FATF · SEC · KYC/AML
